🪶 Game Scroll of Instructions: Warisan Decode

🌺 Welcome, Cultural Seeker!

Begin your journey to uncover Malaysia's hidden heritage and preserve stories time has forgotten.

🗺️ I. Choose a State

Select a Malaysian state to begin. Each state is a level filled with unique heritage tales.

🧭 II. Explore the Map

Receive a Map of Heritage. Choose checkpoints like temples, carvings, and folklore sites to explore.

🧠 III. Solve Challenges

Use your wit to decode riddles and puzzles tied to local traditions and history.

🏅 IV. Earn Culinary Badges

Complete challenges to earn badges — each one a delicious Malaysian dish symbolizing your progress.

🏆 V. Become a Heritage Guardian

Collect all badges to be honored in the Hall of Heritage.

You’re not just playing a game — you’re preserving a nation’s soul.
